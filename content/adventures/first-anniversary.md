One year since three friends walked the Halfmoon Trail and came back as a tribe.

For our first anniversary we traded summit packs for bicycles and gave our knees a weekend off — island roads, white sand, grilled everything, and the full tribe rolling together under the palms with the banner flying.

## Year one, by the numbers

- **8** expeditions, from backyard crags to the roof of the Philippines
- **4** islands: Cebu, Negros, Mindanao, and a beach to celebrate on
- **1** Basic Mountaineering Course held, with a new batch of certified members
- **0** traces left behind

The mountains gave us a lot in our first year. The best thing they gave us was each other.

*Here's to the next climb.*
