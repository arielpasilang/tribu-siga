A tribe that climbs together should train together.

Our first **Basic Mountaineering Course** brought new members and veterans onto the same ground: a full day of instruction covering trip planning, gear and campcraft, trail navigation, wilderness first aid, and — at the heart of everything — the **seven Leave No Trace principles**.

## Why we bother with training

Mountains in the Philippines are generous, but they don't forgive carelessness. Weather turns fast, trails wash out, and help can be a day's hike away. The difference between an epic story and a rescue operation is usually preparation.

BMC 1 ended with certificates, sunburns, and a bigger tribe than we started with. Many of the friends who took this course with us went on to summit Apo and Guiting-Guiting by our side.
