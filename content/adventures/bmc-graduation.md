The best part of running a Basic Mountaineering Course: the day the certificates get handed out.

Under the coconut palms, with the tribe banner spread on the grass, a new batch of members officially joined the ranks of trained Tribu Siga mountaineers. Every certificate in those photos represents a full course of navigation, campcraft, first aid, and Leave No Trace ethics.

## The tribe grows

Some of the faces in this picture had never slept in a tent before joining us. Within a year, several of them had stood on major summits across the Visayas and Mindanao.

That's the whole point. The mountains are better with more friends on them — as long as everyone comes prepared.
