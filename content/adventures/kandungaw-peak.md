You don't always need a boat ticket for a proper adventure. Kandungaw Peak is proof that Cebu keeps world-class scenery in its own backyard.

The peak is a cluster of dramatic **limestone crags** rising out of the Dalaguete highlands — sheer rock faces, knife-edge viewpoints, and drops that make you hold your camera a little tighter. We hiked in on a Saturday afternoon, set camp near the summit, and had the golden hour entirely to ourselves.

## Sunrise service

The morning delivered the classic Cebu highlands show: a sea of clouds filling the valleys below, the crags floating above it like islands, and the early light turning the limestone gold.

An easy weekend, a hard place to leave.
